## 3. Hackathon Discord Submission

**Project Title**: SalesIQ — Enterprise Memory Layer for Sales
**Track**: Track 3: Enterprise Impact (Best Enterprise Use Case)
**Team Size**: 2

**Pitch**:
CRMs remember client contact details, but they forget the actual conversations. SalesIQ acts as the "system of understanding" for sales teams by capturing call audio streams, transcribing them with sub-second latency, and indexing conversational intelligence. 

Leveraging **Gemma 4 31B** hosted on **Cerebras**, we provide live, trigger-based objection handling battlecards *during* active calls, reducing rep ramp times and preventing deal slippage. 

**Core Features**:
* Real-time dialer simulator with active CSS waveform and scroll-synced transcripts.
* Instant objection triggers and buying signal identification.
* Multimodal dropzone: Upload pricing briefs or brochures, and Gemma merges the text context with transcripts to output custom deal briefs and matching next best actions.
* Bi-directional CRM writebacks.
* Fully searchable enterprise database (presets: "Salesforce integration", "pricing questions").

**How to run**:
1. Clone the repository.
2. Run `npm install` and `npm run dev`.
3. Open `http://localhost:3000` to inspect the Landing Page, Live Agent Dashboard, Multimodal Workbench, and Apple-style Presentation deck.
