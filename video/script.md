# SalesIQ 60-Second Launch Video Explainer Script & Storyboard

This document serves as the production-ready script, scene timing, and visual storyboard storyboard for the SalesIQ 60-second video. 

## Video Style
* **Inspiration**: Stripe Sessions, Vercel/Linear launch videos, Apple Keynote.
* **Visuals**: Clean vector layouts, smooth 3D layer parallax, kinetic typography, crisp UI panels. 
* **Audio**: Elegant upbeat ambient electronica with soft syncopated analog synthesizer beats (similar to Stripe Session introductions).
* **Narrator**: Confident, articulate, warm professional voiceover (VO).

## Audio Asset Recommendations
* **Background Track**: "Ambient Pulse" or "Silicon Innovator" (110 BPM, soft electronica featuring piano elements and minor synths).
* **Vocal Quality**: Mid-range baritone, warm, pacing should be slow and deliberate (approximately 130 words in 60 seconds).
